Ulric Todman

Born and raised in NYC, I have worked at a datacenter in Tribeca for the past 12 years.


I've always been interested in coding and computing. My father had an attempt at a tech startup (a virtual trade show platform), and that allowed me to become familiar with the datacenter space. As an adolescent and teen I often attended 2600 meetings and made small html websites in my free time. I am also a US patent holder (Click here for details) I now want to expand my abilities and contribute in a more meaningful and creative way. The ability to code opens a lot of doors in regards to making ideas functional and shareable.
